Deep Learning for the Automated Extraction of Human-Made Surfaces

Dr. Greg Koeln, Radiant Solutions

Place: Exhibit Hall,
Government Pavilion Stage

Human-made surfaces (HMS) are surfaces that are modified by humans and do not have a naturally occurring surface that can be recognized by traditional classification algorithms. Deep learning techniques using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) are adept at utilizing these textures to define categories of features. Dr. Koeln will describe how deep learning will be used to extract HSM features from Sentinel-2 imagery on a global basis and over time.
